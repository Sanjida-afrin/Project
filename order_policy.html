TERMS & CONDITIONS

ORDER POLICY

  1. Orders for less than 100 persons need to be placed (confirmed) by 12n the day prior to the event. Private orders for more than 100 persons need to be placed (confirmed) 3 days in advance of the event. Large orders (300+) need to be confirmed 7 days in advance. (Please note that some menu selections may require additional notice or a substitution may be required. Menu selection for rush orders may be limited, so your flexibility is appreciated).
  2. To confirm the order, a deposit of 50% of the projected total bill needs to be made in advance as mentioned above (as per the cut off time).

CANCELLATIONS & REFUNDS

  1. Cancellations for small orders (less than 100 pax) must reach us in writing (email, Facebook message) at least 24 hrs prior to the scheduled delivery time. Failure to do so will forfeit the advance amount or 50% of the total bill needs to be paid (in case of company payment).
  2. Cancellations for large orders (more than 100) must reach us in writing (email, Facebook message) at least 72 hrs before the event time. Failure to do so will forfeit the advance amount or 50% of the total bill needs to be paid (in case of company payment).
  3. The refund is only available to cancellations compliant with the policies.

PAYMENT

  1. The final bill to be settled on delivery or after completion of the event when the full catering support is ordered.
  2. Corporates with credit agreement on file may be given credit facility for 10 working days after submission of the final invoice.
  3. Govt. orders with tender notice followed by a proper work order can have a credit facility of up to 30 days.

CHECKS (/CHEQUES)

  1. All checks should be made payable to Desh Catering Ltd and money receipt against check will be valid only after the successful realization of the payment.
  2. Advance payment check must be issued 5 working days in advance in order to ensure the successful realization of the amount.

PRICE CHANGES

  1. All prices are subject to change without notice. All food products are chosen based upon availability. We are not responsible for weather changes or price increases due to shortages of power, supply and demand or any unforeseen situation in the food or labor market.

VAT

  1. Applicable VAT to be paid by the client. The GoB requires VAT to be applied to all food & beverage items and service charges at the rate of 7.5%. VAT-exempt organizations must provide documentation to retain on file.

DELIVERY FEE

  1. Delivery charge based on your order volume and location will apply.

(WAITER) SERVICE CHARGES

  1. We can provide professional on-site staff (Waiter & Dish Washer) for your event inside Dhaka at the rate of 750 Taka per staff for a 4-hour period and 1000 Taka per staff for events outside Dhaka. Additional hours will be charged at the rate of 200 Taka per waiter per hour.

GRATUITIES
